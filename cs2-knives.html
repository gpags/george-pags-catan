The Story

I printed my brother's exact Talon for his birthday.

He'd been grinding for that Talon Slaughter for a year. Pulled it from a case the week before his birthday. Float 0.04, almost factory new — and he couldn't shut up about it.

So I screenshotted his inventory, brought the file into the workshop, and printed it. Same blade. Same wear pattern in the paint. Same tiny scratch he kept pointing out. Sanded it for two nights. Hand-painted the splatter. Signed the handle.

He's a grown man. He cried. Held it like it was the real thing.

That's what we do here. You send the screenshot. We print the blade.
