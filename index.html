Me & My Dad

Two hands. One workshop.
No compromises.

It started one winter with a Catan board we wanted but couldn't find. Dad had the printers. I had the patience to redesign the tiles 25 times until they snapped together just right.

One project became five. Catan sets. Collector knives. Anime figures sculpted for shelves. Toy cats in surprise bags. Scratchers our own cats wouldn't ignore.

Every piece is printed, finished, inspected, and shipped by us — father and son — from our home workshop in New Jersey. We answer your emails. We sign the boxes. That's the whole company.
